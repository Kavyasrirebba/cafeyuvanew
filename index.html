Welcome to Cafe Yuva


A safe, vibrant space where today's youth can truly be themselves. At Cafe Yuva, we believe that every teen deserves to be seen, heard, and valued. Whether you're looking for guidance, a place to share your voice, or a spark to ignite your ideas — you've found your tribe.


This is more than a place — it's a movement to bring back the village, where teens are supported by mentors, nurtured through community, and inspired to grow with confidence and grace.

Contact Us

Our Story

"It takes a village to raise a child — but where is the village today?"

Cafe Yuva was born from a deeply personal journey through parenting. In today’s fast-paced, digital world, teens often face invisible emotional challenges and isolation.

When I saw the difference a mentor made in my son’s life — someone outside the family who truly listened — I had an awakening. Our teens don’t just need advice. They need presence, empathy, and safe, caring spaces.

That’s how Cafe Yuva came to life — a place where teens can talk, grow, and just be. And why a "Cafe"? Because it's warm, informal, and welcoming — exactly how we want every young person to feel here.

Our Vision


Imagine a world where every teen feels emotionally safe — where they can share without fear, express without judgment, and be met with genuine understanding.

At Cafe Yuva, we’re building that world. A place where young people are creatively empowered to explore who they are, dream out loud, and take bold steps forward.

Our vision is rooted in the belief that real growth happens when home, school, and society come together to support and uplift our youth. When that happens, we don’t just create confident individuals — we build a stronger, kinder world.

What We Offer


"Support, connection, and inspiration—for every teen and every journey."



SAFE EXPRESSION



A space where teens can speak freely, without fear of being judged or dismissed. We encourage open conversations around identity, emotions, relationships, and aspirations.

MENTORSHIP & COMMUNITY


We connect teens with mentors—caring adults from various walks of life who can guide, inspire, and support them. Sometimes all it takes is one kind word from the right person at the right time.

CREATIVE & ENTREPRENEURIAL EXPLORATION


Cafe Yuva is also a launchpad for ideas—whether it’s a small business concept, a community initiative, or an artistic pursuit. We support youth in bringing their visions to life.

Our Initiatives

Talk Circles

Weekly group sessions where teens come together to share, listen, and connect with one another.

Mentor Meets

Monthly gatherings with inspiring professionals, artists, athletes, and entrepreneurs who offer insights from real-world experience.

Yuva Spark

A creative incubator for teens with ideas—offering guidance on how to plan, pitch, and pursue their projects.

Parent-Teen Dialogues

Facilitated conversations to help bridge communication gaps between parents and children.

WHY CAFE YUVA?


Because it's warm, informal, and welcoming. We want teens to walk in and feel like they belong—no judgments, no pressure—just a space to talk, share, and grow.
